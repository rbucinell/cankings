About:

We recycle all cans and bottles with a NY 5 cent deposit. All brands, sizes, and amounts accepted. We also help to support many Schools, sports, clubs, and individuals. Everyone is welcomed to set up accounts and fundraisers at Can Kings.

Here is how it works:

Bring us your recyclables and we will hand count your bottles and cans and pay you (the customers) cash on the spot! The service is quick, easy, clean, and honest!
